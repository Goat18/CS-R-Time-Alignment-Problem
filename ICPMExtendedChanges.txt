Here is a summary of the changes made to the extended journal version of the Timed Alignments paper. 

1. The abstract, the introduction, the conclusion, and the title of section 7 have been edited to better reflect the actual contributions and focus of our paper. 

2. The introduction now clearly states that this is an extension of the existing conference version, and the changes in this paper have been highlighted. 

3. The preliminaries have been edited to be easier to grasp, and typographical errors have been addressed. 

4. The implementation section has been removed. 

5. A related work section has been added, which details some other approaches to distances for timed words, and alignments over time-series. 

6. Language has been adjusted throughout to address most of the ambiguities mentioned. 

7. (To do) Improved the running examples to provide more context for the design choices, and better readability.
